ABOUT BOXER

The Boxer is a medium to large, short-haired dog breed of mastiff-type, developed in Germany. The coat is smooth and tight-fitting; colors are fawn, brindled, or white, with or without white markings. The Boxer's short coat simplifies grooming, a practical feature for the Indian weather.
